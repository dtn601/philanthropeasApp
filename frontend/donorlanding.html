TIPS

What to Donate:

  • Peanut butter, nut butters
  • Canned meats, including fish
  • Hearty soups
  • Low/no sodium canned vegetables
  • Pre-packaged snacks for a kid
  • Canned goods with pull of lids
  • Food that requires minmal prep
  • A check is always a good donation--charities can buy the exact food they need in buld at a lower cost.

What Not to Donate:

  • Food that is expired
  • Food that is open
  • Food that is packaged in glass or otherwise fragile
  • Food that is perishable (unless the charity says otherwise)
  • Alcohol
